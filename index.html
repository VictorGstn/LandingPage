About Us

The Nuka-Cola Corporation entered the beverage market in 2044, with the invention of the soft drink by John-Caleb Bradberton after two years of experimentation. We took the United States market by storm and within a year all of our products could be purchased nationwide. Our popularity rapidly increased and within several years we are the most popular beverage across the globe.
